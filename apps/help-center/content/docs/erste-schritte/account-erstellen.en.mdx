This guide walks you through creating your GoodFunds account and setting up your organization. All you
need is an email address and a few minutes.

## Before you start

Have these ready:

- an **email address** you can access (ideally an official address of your organization),
- the **name of your organization**.

<Callout type="info">
  You'll create your personal account first, then set up your organization. You can invite the rest
  of your team later — see [Invite your team](./team-einladen).
</Callout>

## Create your account

<Steps>

<Step>

### Open the registration page

Go to [manager.goodfunds.de](https://manager.goodfunds.de) and click **Create account**.

![The GoodFunds registration page](/docs-assets/erste-schritte/account-erstellen-start.png)

> 📸 *Screenshot to be added.*

</Step>

<Step>

### Enter your details

Enter your **name**, your **email address**, and a **secure password**. Confirm that you've read the
terms of use, then click **Register**.

<Callout type="warn">
  Choose a password with at least 12 characters that you don't use anywhere else. Even better: turn
  on [two-factor authentication](/docs/sicherheit/zwei-faktor-authentifizierung) right after your
  first login.
</Callout>

</Step>

<Step>

### Confirm your email

We'll send you an email with a confirmation link. Open it and click the link to confirm your address.

> No email? Check your spam folder and wait a minute or two. You can request the confirmation again
> from the login page.

</Step>

<Step>

### Set up your organization

After your first login we'll ask for your **organization's name**. Enter it — this is the name your
supporters will see later. You can change it anytime in the settings.

</Step>

</Steps>

## You're in — what's next?

You're all set. Your dashboard is the starting point for everything else.

## Frequently asked questions

**I registered but can't log in.**
Check whether you've confirmed your email address. Login is blocked until you do.

**Can I manage more than one organization?**
Yes. Get in touch with support and we'll set up access to additional organizations for you.

**I forgot my password.**
Click **Forgot password** on the login page and follow the instructions in the email.
